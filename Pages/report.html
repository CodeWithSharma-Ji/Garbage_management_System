Frequently Asked Questions

When will the garbage be collected?

Our team typically arrives within 30-45 minutes for regular reports and 15-20 minutes for high-priority emergency reports. Response time depends on vehicle availability in your area.

Do I need to be present?

For door-to-door collections, it's helpful if you're available. For street garbage reports, you don't need to wait. Our team will handle cleanup once they arrive.

Can I track the garbage vehicle?

Yes! After submission, you'll receive tracking information via our system. You can monitor the vehicle's location in real-time until it reaches your area.

What if I have multiple problem areas?

Submit separate reports for each location. This helps us prioritize and assign the appropriate resources to each area effectively.

Is this service free?

Yes! Reporting garbage in your area is completely free for all citizens. It's our commitment to maintaining clean, healthy neighborhoods for everyone.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

Select "High" garbage level for emergencies. For urgent situations like hazardous materials, also call our 24/7 helpline for immediate assistance.

Reporting Guidelines

What Should You Report?

  • Overflowing garbage bins or containers
  • Garbage scattered on streets or public areas
  • Illegal dumping of waste
  • Foul odors indicating decomposing garbage
  • Damaged or broken garbage collection containers
  • Hazardous waste or suspicious materials
  • Any cleanliness or sanitation concerns in your area

How to Help Us Help You Better?

  • Provide accurate location information
  • Be specific about the type and volume of garbage
  • Mention any special hazards or concerns
  • Describe landmarks nearby if the location is hard to find
  • Include your contact number for follow-up if needed
  • Provide feedback after service for continuous improvement

Important Notes

  • Please don't attempt to handle hazardous or unknown waste materials
  • Do not block access to reported garbage areas for the collection team
  • Provide factual information to help us prioritize resources effectively
  • Multiple reports from the same area are compiled for efficient routing
  • Your privacy is protected - personal details are used only for service delivery
