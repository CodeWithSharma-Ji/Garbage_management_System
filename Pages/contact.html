Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

Phone inquiries are attended immediately during office hours. Email responses and contact form submissions are addressed within 24 hours of receipt.

What if I need emergency support?

Call our 24/7 helpline for emergency situations. Sanitation crew is available 24/7 for urgent garbage collection and cleanup needs.

Can I lodge a complaint?

Yes! Use our dedicated complaint email (complaints@ecomgmt.com) or call the helpline. We investigate and resolve issues within 48 hours.

Is there a complaint resolution timeline?

We commit to resolving 95% of complaints within 48 hours. Complex issues may take up to 5 business days. We'll keep you updated throughout.

Do you have a grievance redressal officer?

Yes. For unresolved grievances, contact our Grievance Officer at grievance@ecomgmt.com or through the main helpline.

How can I provide feedback?

We value your feedback! Send suggestions to feedback@ecomgmt.com or use the feedback option in your account after each service.
